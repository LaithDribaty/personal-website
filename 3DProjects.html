    house project

    In my second year in college I was playing a game called "return to raverhearst", this game inspired me to take the idea of moving between places like you hover over a door and then press to enter the room so I took photos of my country house and then made a website that act exactly like the game, we move around in the house by pressing doors or some areas, the project was my first thoughts of VR and benefits of VR and the great experience that VR can give.

  • Moving to the Web

    semester project

    In the second half of the same year we have a semester project, so we decided to try to make an online judge (like codeforces, hackerrank, lightoj, SPOJ..) because we were ACMers (ICPC contesters) so we wanted to know how all of this work but to make work more fantasy or more fun, we decided to make it a 3D game that you walk along some rooms and then solve problems and then if it is right the doors open.
